# CR7 Algorithm Quest - Asset Manifest

Place the production assets in the paths below.

## Audio

### BGM

- `audio/bgm/main_menu.mp3` - Calming classic football game menu music.
- `audio/bgm/gameplay_search.mp3` - Techno/synth tension track during active search stepping.
- `audio/bgm/stadium_crowd_loop.wav` - Ambient low-volume crowd murmur for the background.

### SFX

- `audio/sfx/button_click.wav` - Retro click when selecting algorithms or clicking buttons.
- `audio/sfx/cell_step.wav` - Short synth beep/wood block sound as a cell is evaluated.
- `audio/sfx/danger_trigger.wav` - Harsh buzz/alarm sound when landing on -20 fire or -5 danger.
- `audio/sfx/level_up.wav` - Triumph fanfare or whistle with gold coin sound effect.
- `audio/sfx/local_maxima.wav` - Thud/error buzzer when Simple HC hits a dead end.
- `audio/sfx/game_over.wav` - Disappointed sigh or slow whistle sound effect.
- `audio/sfx/siuuu.wav` - CR7 celebration sound when reaching the World Cup trophy.

## Fonts

- `fonts/pixel_primary.ttf` - Medium sans-serif pixel font for titles, stats, and dialogs.
- `fonts/pixel_bold.ttf` - Heavy bold block font for Level Up screens and UI numbers.
